Golgusa Temple

This page features maps of one of my favorite places in the world, Golgusa Temple in South Korea.

 


Golgusa is a Buddhist Temple I once stayed stayed at. The mountains were stunning, the monks were kind and provided excellent meditation and mindfulness education, and the temple grounds were incredibly peaceful.
